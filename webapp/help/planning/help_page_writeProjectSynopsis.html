Write project synopsis

Your project synopsis is an agreement on what your project will be about (and for). It's also a short "elevator speech" you can use to describe -- in a sentence or two -- what you are doing and why you are doing it.

What to do here

In a few sentences, summarize everything you've decided on in the planning phase of your project. Write a summary that will get people excited to participate in your project. (If you can't, do you need to do more planning?)

Connections to other pages

The information you enter here will appear in the project report.
