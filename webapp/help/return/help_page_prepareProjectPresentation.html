Prepare outline of project presentation

You can use this page to build a simple outline for a presentation in which you explain your project to other people (your funders, your colleagues, your community).

Note that this page is more of a placeholder for future functionality than it is an actual attempt at functionality. The main reason to include it in NarraFirma is that if you do create a project presentation, you will have saved at least its outline in your NarraFirma project for later reference.

What to do here

Think about the points you want to make in your presentation. For each, answer the questions. You can copy story excerpts from elsewhere and paste them here.

Connections to other pages

This page is for your records only. Using it has no effect on any other page in the application.

Frequently-asked questions

Can I include graphs created during the project?

No. As it stands now, this page is far too simple to support that. If you want to include graphs in your presentation, you can describe them here. But you will need to add them to your presentation outside of NarraFirma. To do that, create a catalysis report, print it, and copy any images you want to use into your presentation.
